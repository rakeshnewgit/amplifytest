TO SIGN IN…

  1. FIND YOUR NAME ABOVE.
  2. CLICK THE LINK HEADING THE COLUMN IN WHICH YOUR NAME APPEARS


  • Use your enterprise credentials if challenged.

If you are not passed directly through to the AWS Console:

  • Select the Platform_Developer role on the relevant account,
    then click the Sign in button.

After the AWS Management Console loads,verify that your AWS account region is set to US East (N. Virginia). You can find this in the right upper corner next to the Role/username @ AWS-account listing. Click on the region and select US East (N. Virginia).

That's it! Access the relevant service per instructions below.

Note that your credentials will time out after approximately 15 minutes of inactivity in the console.

When this happens, ignore the Reload prompt and instead simply point your browser again to https://go.accenture.com/awsgo/ and sign back in to the account you were signed-in to previously.

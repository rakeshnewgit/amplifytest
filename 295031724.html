New IT Delivery Patterns : AWS Analytics 2 - Step 4: Create Datasource in QuickSight

Created by Karina Jonane, last modified by Thomas R. Evans on May 21, 2021

This step shows you how to use AWS QuickSight to connect to your database (DB) and quickly visualize your dataset with the AWS QuickSight Service.

What is QuickSight?  QuickSight is an easy-to-use, cloud-based business analytics tool that makes it easy to build visualizations.  Its features are not as powerful (yet) as tools like Tableau and Splunk and its biggest weakness is its limited ability to share and export outside of AWS.  But ease of use is a major feature and expect AWS to continue rolling out additional capabilities.

Learn more about AWS QuickSight.


Access QuickSight

  1. Click on Services in the left upper corner of the page. In the drop-down list of all available services, choose (click on) QuickSight, or type QuickSight in quick search field.

  2. If you are not recognized as among the CMT QuickSight account users, you will be redirected to sign-up as an account user. You will need to enter your enterprise email to confirm you are added. Then click Continue.
    Note:  When returning to QuickSight at a later date, you may need to repeat this process, as CMT periodically removes names from the QuickSight user list to help manage costs.

TIP

Access to QuickSight requires that you are signed in to AWS. If your session expires...
1. Log into go.accenture.com/awsgo/.
2. Choose QuickSight from all services list (or search for it via the quick search field).
3. This will automatically redirect you to the service.

Create a new data set with Athena as the data source

  1. Confirm your selected region (located in the right upper corner) is US East (N. Virginia).

  2. Click New analysis in the upper-right corner of the QuickSight screen.
  3. Click to create a New data set, then click to select Athena from among the list of available New Data Sources.

  4. Give your data source a name you will recognize, leave Athena workgroup unchanged, then click Validate connection.


  5. After validation completes, click Create data source.
  6. Choose the database you created when you configured the crawler's output and select the product_marketing_data table, then click Edit/Preview data.
  7. This is your pending analysis. Confirm that Direct query is the selected mode in the upper left-hand corner of the preview page, rename the analysis (optional),  then click Save & visualize.

You are now ready to create visualizations of the analysis.
